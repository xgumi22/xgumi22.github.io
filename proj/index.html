UNIVERSITY STUDENT COUNCIL

The University Student Council (USC) is the highest student organization of the University of Makati (UMak). It designed activities that will surely enhance the knowledge and creativity of the UMak students, promote and protect their rights and welfare, foster spiritual brotherhood, and instill the national conciousness among them that will ultimately benefit the organization.
